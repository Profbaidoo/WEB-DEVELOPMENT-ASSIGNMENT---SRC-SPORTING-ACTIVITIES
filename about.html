About SRC Sports Academy

The Student Representative Council (SRC) of Pentecost University has launched a new initiative known as the SRC Sports Academy, aimed at enhancing the university's reputation while promoting sports development within the university and the surrounding community. The academy has secured funding from community sources and sports bodies, expanding its offerings to include wrestling, taekwondo, judo, and volleyball alongside existing programs like football, rugby, ballet, table tennis, handball, and gymnastics.

Mission

Develop disciplined student-athletes through structured coaching and mentorship.

Vision

Become a leading regional sports institution recognized for excellence and community impact.

Values

Integrity, teamwork, inclusion, and continuous growth in sport and academics.
